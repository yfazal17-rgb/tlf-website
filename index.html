ABOUT.TXT
——————————————————————————————————————————

The Life Folder started with a simple idea:
your wardrobe is an archive.

Not of trends — of moments.
The hoodie that got you through a hard winter.
The shirt you were wearing when something changed.
The thing you picked up because it just felt right.

We're building a gender-neutral clothing brand
that believes in making things that mean something.
Slow. Intentional. For keeps.

We're just getting started.
No products yet — just a name, a vision,
and a folder full of things we want to make.
We think that's enough for now.

— TLF

The Life Folder
